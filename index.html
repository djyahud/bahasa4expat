Courses

Our courses are designed to develop proficiency and communication in Indonesian language.
The courses are designed not only to learn Bahasa Indonesia, but also to grasp cultural practices in Indonesia.

Emphasis is placed on the functional use of the language to enable students to participate in basic
conversational exchanges, understand native speakers of Indonesian language, complete written
compositions describing daily events or personal experiences, and to read basic texts on familiar topics
such as weather, seasons, food, family, studies and friends
